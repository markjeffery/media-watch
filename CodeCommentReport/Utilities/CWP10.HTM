Remarks:
LTC includes date / time (HH:MM:SS) and frame (FF) For convenience, FF is encoded as FF*10 milliseconds. For example frame 1 is 10 milliseconds, frame 20 is 200 milliseconds. This is OK for comparison, but no good for direct maths! Also includes setting for frames per second (fps) Use 25 for 625 line and 30 for 525 line
